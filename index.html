7 X GURLS

7 X GURLS is a sensational music duo formed in 2022 by two extraordinarily talented cousins, Lia and Jay. Hailing from the enchanting state of Wisconsin, USA, Lia and Jay embarked on their musical journey with a shared dream of creating captivating music that resonates with audiences around the world.

From a young age, Lia and Jay exhibited an immense passion for music. Growing up in a musically inclined family, they were exposed to various genres and artists, which ignited their love for creating and performing music. Inspired by their favorite artists and driven by their own natural talents, Lia and Jay began crafting their unique sound, combining elements of pop, R&B, and soul.

Throughout their early years, Lia and Jay honed their skills by participating in talent shows, local gigs, and open mic nights. They tirelessly worked on improving their vocal abilities, stage presence, and songwriting prowess. Their unwavering dedication to their craft soon caught the attention of music industry professionals, who recognized the duo's exceptional talent and potential.

In 2022, the world was introduced to 7 X GURLS, a musical force to be reckoned with. With their mesmerizing harmonies, powerful vocals, and an undeniable chemistry on stage, Lia and Jay quickly captured the hearts of music lovers worldwide. Their debut performances were met with standing ovations, and the duo became known for their electrifying live shows.

As songwriters, Lia and Jay infuse their own personal experiences, emotions, and storytelling into their music. Their lyrics delve into themes of love, empowerment, self-discovery, and the human condition. With each song, they aim to connect deeply with their audience, offering messages of hope, strength, and resilience.

Over the years, 7 X GURLS have released a string of chart-topping hits that have solidified their status as one of the industry's most beloved acts. From infectious up-tempo tracks that get audiences on their feet to heartfelt ballads that tug at the heartstrings, Lia and Jay consistently deliver music that resonates with listeners of all ages.
