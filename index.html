7 X GURLS

7 X GURLS is a music band created in 2022 by two cousins, Lia and Jay. The duo, known for their exceptional vocal abilities and songwriting skills, captivate audiences with their powerful performances and heartfelt lyrics.

History

Formation and Early Years

Lia and Jay, both gifted singers and talented musicians, joined forces in 2022 to form 7 X GURLS. The duo quickly established themselves as a prominent musical act, despite consisting of only two members. With their incredible voices and passion for music, Lia and Jay create a dynamic and captivating experience for their listeners.

Not only do Lia and Jay possess great vocal abilities, but they also showcase their talent as songwriters. They collaborate to write and compose their own songs, infusing their unique perspectives and experiences into their music.

Rise to Fame

In a short period of time, 7 X GURLS gained recognition for their exceptional performances and original compositions. Audiences were drawn to their powerful vocals and heartfelt lyrics. Their debut single, "Voices of the Heart," released in 2023, quickly garnered attention and propelled them into the spotlight.

7 X GURLS continues to captivate audiences with their electrifying performances and their ability to connect with listeners through their music. They have gained a dedicated fan base and are poised for continued success in the music industry.
